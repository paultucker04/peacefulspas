About Us

About Peaceful Spas

Peaceful Spas is family owned local business operating out of Palm Bay, Florida. We have been operating since 1999. We seek out used spas and refurbish them to give them a second life. Our refurbished spas rival the quality of one that is brand new. The owner, Chad Peace, has been working on spas for over 20 years

We custom build our spas to meet our customers exact needs. Brand new spas don't offer the level of customization that we can provide. They certainly can't touch our prices either.

I just really like working on spas.

Chad Peace Owner
