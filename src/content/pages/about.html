About Peaceful Spas

Peaceful Spas is family owned local business operating out of Palm Bay, Florida. We have been operating since 1999. We seek out used spas and refirbish them to give them a second life. Our refirbished spas rival the quality of one that is brand new. The owner, Chad Peace, has been working on spas for over 20 years

We custom build our spas to meet our customers exact needs. Brand new spas don't offer the level of customization that we can provide. They certainly can't touch our prices either.

I just really like working on spas.

Chad Peace Owner

About Chad Peace

Chad Peace is the owner of Peaceful Spas. He started working on spas as a hobby in 1999. In 2002, he founded Peaceful Spas and was able to transition to working on spas full time. He is passionate about spas and finds a lot of joy in working on and customizing them. He is from Palm Bay Florida and loves serving his local community.
